Called to Serve

At St. Patrick's, we believe that every person has been gifted by God with unique talents and called to serve. Our various ministries provide opportunities for spiritual growth, fellowship, and service to our parish and community. Whether you're drawn to worship, education, outreach, or fellowship, there's a place for you to make a difference.

Worship & Liturgical Ministries

Music Ministry

Share your musical gifts through our adult choir, children's choir, contemporary music group, or as a cantor. No audition required - just a heart for worship!

Meeting Times: Choir rehearsal Wednesdays 7:00 PM

Contact: Sarah Mitchell, Director of Music

Extraordinary Ministers of Holy Communion
