Parish Leadership Structure

Pastoral Team

The Pastoral team consists of the clergy (the Parish Priest and Associate) and religious sisters (Sisters of Charity) who provide spiritual guidance and pastoral care to our community.

  • Parish Priest: Very Rev. Fr. Michael O'Brien
  • Associate Pastor: Rev. Fr. James Adebayo
  • Religious Sister: Sr. Mary Theresa, RGS

Parish Pastoral Council

The Parish Pastoral Council (PPC) is constituted by the Parish Priest to help in advising and articulating policies and programmes for the parish.

  • Chairperson: Mr. Peter Okafor
  • Secretary: Mrs. Grace Adebayo
  • Members: Representatives from all parish groups

Parish Laity Council

The Parish Laity Council consists of the lay faithful representing the pious societies in view of discussing and putting forward the needs of the lay faithful.

  • Chairman: Mr. Emmanuel Nwosu
  • Vice-Chairman: Mrs. Blessing Ojo
  • Representatives from all lay organizations

Parish Societies

Traditional

Traditional
