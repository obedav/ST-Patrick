Message from Our Parish Priest

Very Rev. Fr. Michael O'Brien

"Dear brothers and sisters in Christ, welcome to our parish family! At St. Patrick's, we strive to be a beacon of hope and love in our community. Whether you are a longtime parishioner or visiting for the first time, know that you are cherished and welcomed here. Together, let us grow in faith, serve one another, and spread the Gospel message throughout Lagos and beyond."
