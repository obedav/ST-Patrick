Our Story

A Journey of Excellence Since 1949

üì∏ Historical Photo - School in 1949

St. Patrick's Catholic Nursery and Primary School Igbogila Ipaja, Lagos started in the year 1949 by Rev. Fr. Oflin. Fr. Oflin, seeing children moving about with nothing to do other than playing around, began bringing them together to teach them.

These children were the indigenes of Igbogila and Ayobo. He made use of a mud and thatched roof building. At that time, the population grew to fifty pupils with five teachers headed by Mr. Bankole E.B.

The Great Transformation (2005)

The greatest transformation of the school came with the elevation of St. Patrick's Catholic Parish on November 27, 2005, which brought in the first pioneer Priest, Rev. Fr. Paschal Obioma CSSp. He started constructing a two-story building which he completed in less than one year.
