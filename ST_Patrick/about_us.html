Our History

The first Community service was held in 1965 with 80 faithful Catholics in attendance at a small chapel on Victoria Island. What began as a modest gathering of devoted believers has grown into one of Lagos's most vibrant Catholic parishes.

The journey began when a small group of Catholic families recognized the need for a parish to serve the growing Catholic community in the heart of Lagos. Through prayers, dedication, and the grace of God, St. Patrick's Catholic Church was established to serve the spiritual needs of Catholics across Lagos and beyond.

The labours and fruits of that little gathering of enthusiastic Catholics over five decades ago is currently being enjoyed as a great parish ‚Äì St. Patrick's Catholic Church Lagos. The journey has been long and blessed, but the results have been spiritually fulfilling for thousands of souls.

Key Milestones

  • 1965: First Mass celebrated in temporary chapel
  • 1968: Construction of permanent church building
  • 1975: Parish officially established by Lagos Archdiocese
  • 1985: Church expansion and renovation
  • 2000: Modern parish complex completed
  • 2015: Golden Jubilee celebration - 50 years of faith
  • 2025: Diamond Jubilee preparation - 60 years of service

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be a welcoming Catholic community that proclaims the Gospel of Jesus Christ, celebrates the sacraments, serves those in need, and forms disciples who transform the world through faith, hope, and love.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant parish family where every member grows in holiness, discovers their calling, and actively participates in the mission of Christ throughout Lagos and beyond.
